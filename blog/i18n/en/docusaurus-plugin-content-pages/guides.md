## Installing UltraViewer (Remote Support Software)

UltraViewer allows PCS technicians to connect to your computer and provide remote support.

### Step 1 — Download

Visit [ultraviewer.net](https://ultraviewer.net) → go to the **Download** tab → click the **Download** button.

Or download directly here:

👉 [Download UltraViewer](https://dl2.ultraviewer.net/UltraViewer_setup_6.6_vi.exe)

### Step 2 — Install

Once downloaded, open the `.exe` installer → click **Next** and follow the on-screen instructions to complete installation.

### Step 3 — Share your ID & Password

After installation, open UltraViewer. You will see your **Your ID** and **Password** — send these to the PCS technician via WhatsApp or email to begin your support session.
