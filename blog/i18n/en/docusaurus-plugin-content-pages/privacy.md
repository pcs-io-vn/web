# Privacy Policy
**PCS Vietnam** · pcs.io.vn · info@pcs.io.vn  
Last updated: March 2026

---

## 1. Information We Collect

When you subscribe to and use PCS Vietnam services, we only collect information necessary to provide the service:

- **Personal information**: Full name, email address, phone number
- **Device information**: Device type, operating system, issue description (for technical support purposes)
- **Transaction information**: Payment history, subscription cycle

We do **not** collect passwords, bank card details, or other sensitive personal data.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

Collected information is used for the following purposes:

- Activating and managing your service plan
- Scheduling and providing technical support
- Sending notifications about service status, renewals, or changes to terms
- Processing refund requests or complaints

---

## 3. Information Sharing

Your information is **not shared, sold, or rented** to third parties for commercial purposes.

Information may only be disclosed if:
- Required by law enforcement under Vietnamese law
- You have given explicit written consent

---

## 4. Data Storage & Security

- Information is stored in internal service management tools (email, calendar, support notes)
- We apply reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access
- Data is retained for the duration of your subscription and for a maximum of **1 year** after the service ends

---

## 5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access**: Request to view the information we hold about you
- **Correction**: Request updates to inaccurate information
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of all personal data

To exercise these rights, contact: [info@pcs.io.vn](mailto:info@pcs.io.vn)

---

## 6. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated. Existing customers will be notified via email at least **14 days** before changes take effect.
