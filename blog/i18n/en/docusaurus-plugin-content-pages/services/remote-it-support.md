# 🖥️ Remote IT Support

Get your computer fixed from home — no need to go anywhere.

---

## How it works

### Step 1 — Remote connection
We connect to your computer using professional remote support tools to diagnose and resolve issues directly.

> 👉 Don't have the software? See the [installation guide](/en/guides) or download from the [Resources page](/en/resources).

### Step 2 — Issue resolution
Our technician resolves the issue directly on your machine. Based on our experience, **80% of issues are resolved within 60 minutes**.

### Step 3 — Pickup (if needed)
If the issue cannot be resolved remotely (e.g. hardware failure), we will arrange to **pick up your device** for hands-on inspection and repair.

### Step 4 — Return via Grab
Once repaired and fully tested, your device is **delivered back to you via Grab**.

> ⚠️ Pickup & return service is only available within **Ho Chi Minh City**.
