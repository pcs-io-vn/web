# 🔧 Hardware Repair & Replacement

Computer won't boot, screen flickering, fan making strange noises? We diagnose and fix it fast.

## Common issues we handle

- 💾 HDD/SSD failure, slow performance, data loss
- 🧠 RAM errors, system freezes, blue screen (BSOD)
- 🔋 Battery drain, won't charge, swollen battery
- 🖥️ Screen distortion, no display, backlight failure
- 🌀 Loud fan noise, overheating

## How it works

### Step 1 — Remote assessment
We first connect remotely to assess the device condition.

### Step 2 — Pickup
If physical inspection is needed, we arrange **on-site device pickup** at a time convenient for you.

### Step 3 — Diagnosis & quote
Our technician performs a thorough inspection and provides a **detailed quote**. We only proceed after receiving your **approval**.

### Step 4 — Repair & return
Once repaired and fully tested, your device is **delivered back via Grab**.

> ⚠️ Pickup & return service is only available within **Ho Chi Minh City**.
